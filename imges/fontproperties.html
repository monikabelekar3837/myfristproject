Swami Samartha left his mortal coil in 1878, but his legacy continues to inspire and guide people around the world. His teachings and spiritual practices continue to be taught and followed by his followers,and his ashram in Akkalkot remains a place of pilgrimage for many devotees. Shri Swami Samarth also known as Swami of Akkalkot was an Indian spiritual master of the Dattatreya Tradition. He is a widely known spiritual figure in various Indian states including Karnataka and Maharashtra, Swami Bhakts are spreading all over the world, in Maharashtra Every small areas now has Swami Math where Swami's Devotees sit and worship.
